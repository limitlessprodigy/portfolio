Poetz Realm

A Weekly Testing Stage For Local Creatives

Poetz Realm was the main event created for the Push The Culture campaign. With GOATT United's local production house we created a space for young creatives to test out their skills on stage and behind the camera. Each week I would coordinate this event as a masterclass to teach local creatives how to perform in front of cameras, and would direct content creators on how to put together client ready media around live audiences.

Poetz Realm was used as a creative hub to create community with the local creatives while also creating professional grade content for local artists to establish their brand in the area.
